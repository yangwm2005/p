Sync Between Devices

How to sync your data across devices:

  1. Generate a shareable link with the "Generate Share Link" button
  2. The link is automatically copied to your clipboard
  3. Send the link to yourself via email, messaging app, or any other method
  4. On your other device, open the link to automatically load your data
  5. Alternatively, use the file method to download and transfer the file
